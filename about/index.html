Jann is a software aficionado by night and a nameless writer by day interested in computers and publishing, respectively.

He likes to play with words, data structures, algorithms, and file formats, emphasising on correct logic and readable code.

Apart from running a software consultancy, Jann works at Dentsu Aegis Network as a public relations executive—drafting, copy-checking, and revising formatted text meant for print or speech.

His portfolio has seen the likes of Microsoft, Dell EMC, Micromax, Airtel, Revlon, Durex, and Careems.
